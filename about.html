About Pulse

Every month, around 10 million dance parties take place worldwide. We believe that by making these parties even better, more people would dance and experience greater joy.

Pulse aims to make parties significantly better by using AI and related technologies.

Our first goal is to help DJs become more creative in selecting their next track, using AI that learns from the collective experiences of all Pulse users.

We've added tools like the Pulse Hot 100, which shows the top songs at dance parties in your area, and QR codes that allow your audience to request songs before the party.

We're constantly adding new features to keep things exciting.

Getting started

Install Pulse on the MacBook you use for your parties.

You can instantly load tracks into Serato or other supported DJ software, and Pulse will offer recommendations based on AI analysis of what DJs have recently played in your area.

Within 15-20 minutes of installation, Pulse will analyze your history and start suggesting tracks you typically play next, including those you might have forgotten.

System requirements

  • Operating System: macOS 11 and upwards. A Windows build is available via the Microsoft Store.
  • Supported DJ software: Serato, Rekordbox, Virtual DJ, Traktor, djay Pro.
