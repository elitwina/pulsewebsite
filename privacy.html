Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") governs the manner in which Sonic Beat Ltd., (the "Company", "Pulse", "We", or "Us"), collects, uses, stores, and discloses information collected from users ("User" or "You") of the Pulse DJ desktop application (the "Application").

Last updated: 15 June 2026

This Policy forms an integral part of the Terms of Use applicable to the Application. By using the Application, You consent to the collection, processing, and use of Your information in accordance with this Policy. If You do not agree to this Policy, You should not use, and are free to discontinue using, the Application.

1. Definitions

For the purpose of this Policy:

Application
The Pulse DJ desktop software, including all related features, content, and services offered thereunder.
Personal Data
Information relating to an identified or identifiable User, including an email address, password, and any associated usage data that can be reasonably linked to such User.
Play History
Data relating to the tracks and playlists played by a User on compatible DJ platforms, including but not limited to Serato, Virtual DJ, RekordBox, Traktor, and djay Pro.
Logs and Diagnostics
Usage information, technical logs, and error reports collected automatically by the Application for maintenance, diagnostics, and performance monitoring purposes.
Anonymized Data
Data that has been processed in such a way that it cannot reasonably be used to identify any specific User, including aggregated play history used for improving AI models.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

Upon registration, We collect the User's email address and password for the purpose of account creation and management. No other personally identifiable information (such as name) is required.

2.2 Play History

We collect Play History data relating to the tracks and playlists the User plays on supported DJ platforms.

This data may exist in two forms:

  • Identifiable Data, which is associated with a User account and used only to provide personalized song recommendations.
  • Anonymized Data, which is stripped of identifying information and used to train and improve Pulse DJ's AI recommendation systems.

2.3 Logs and Diagnostics

We collect application usage data, including logs, crash reports, and performance statistics, solely for the purpose of diagnostics, troubleshooting, and product improvement.

3. Use of Information

The Company uses collected data solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the functionality of the Application.
  • To enable personalized features such as song recommendations.
  • To enhance and train AI systems using anonymized Play History data.
  • To send account-related notifications and administrative communications.
  • To send promotional or product-related messages to the User's registered email address (Users may unsubscribe at any time).

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The Company does not sell, rent, or share any Personal Data, including email addresses or Play History, with any third party, whether directly or indirectly.

Anonymized or aggregated Play History data may be used for analytical or system improvement purposes, provided that such data cannot reasonably be used to identify any User.

5. Data Retention

The Company retains collected data, including Personal Data, Play History, and Logs, indefinitely at this time, unless deletion is requested by the User under Section 6 below. Anonymized Data used for AI training or analytics may be retained permanently, as it cannot be linked to any identifiable individual.

6. User Rights and Data Deletion

Users may request the deletion of their personally identifiable data (such as email address, password, and identifiable play history) by contacting Us at support@pulsedj.com.

Upon verification of the request, We will remove all such data from Our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

Anonymized Data, which cannot be used to identify an individual User, cannot be deleted, as it no longer constitutes Personal Data under this Policy.

7. Data Security

The Company implements industry-standard security measures, including encryption using key and salt, to protect stored data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

While We take reasonable precautions, no system or method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and We cannot guarantee absolute security of Your data.

8. International Data Transfers

User data may be stored or processed in data centers located in the United States and Israel, utilizing secure infrastructure services provided by Amazon Web Services (AWS) and MongoDB Atlas.

By using the Application, You acknowledge and agree that Your information may be transferred to, and maintained on, servers located outside Your country of residence, subject to this Policy.

9. Children's Privacy

The Application is not age-restricted and may be used by individuals of any age. However, the Company does not knowingly collect Personal Data from minors under the age of 13. If You believe that a child has provided Personal Data to Us without parental consent, please contact support@pulsedj.com, and We will take appropriate action.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

The Company may modify or update this Policy from time to time by posting a revised version to the Application. Such changes shall take effect immediately upon posting. Continued use of the Application after such posting constitutes the User's acceptance of the updated Policy.

11. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Israel, and the competent courts in Tel Aviv, Israel, shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Policy.

12. Contact Information

For any questions, concerns, or requests relating to this Policy or the handling of Your data, please contact Us at: support@pulsedj.com
