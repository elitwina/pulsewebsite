Frequently asked

What is PulseDJ?

Pulse is a DJ copilot for macOS and Windows. It watches what you are playing in your DJ software and suggests what to play next, based on what thousands of DJs have played in similar situations. A second brain that knows what works on the dancefloor.

Is Pulse free? How does Pulse make money?

Every feature is available in the Free plan. Pro plans add speed, remove limits, and help fund ongoing development. Pulse is funded by Zeev Ventures from California, which gives us the privilege of operating for a few years as we build value for you.

Which DJ software is supported by Pulse?

Serato, Rekordbox, Traktor, Virtual DJ, and djay Pro. Pulse runs alongside the platform you already use - no migration, no library changes.

Which operating systems does Pulse support?
  • Mac: macOS 11 or later (Big Sur, Monterey, Ventura, Sonoma, Sequoia). Native on Apple Silicon and Intel.
  • Windows: Windows 10 or later, installed via the Microsoft Store.
Do I need internet at the gig?

You can use Pulse at parties without an internet connection. You only need a network connection during the first hour or so - Pulse analyzes your history and loads a local database so you can use it offline later. When you do have an internet connection during a party, it can improve the suggestions you get.

Will Pulse change any of my crates, playlists, or track files?

No. Pulse only reads your library and history data - it never modifies your audio files, crates, or playlists.

Can I run Pulse on multiple computers?

Yes. Install Pulse on as many machines as you like and sign in with the same account on each. Your full playing history syncs across devices so your personalized MyStyle suggestions follow you everywhere.
